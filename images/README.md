# Image Setup Guide

## Required Images for Splash Auto Detail Website

Place your images in this folder following the naming convention below.

---

## 1. Hero Background Image

**File:** `hero-car.jpg`  
**Size:** 1920x1080px (minimum) - larger is fine  
**Purpose:** Background image for the hero section (top of page)  
**Content:** Your best, cleanest detailed car - show off your best work!

**Tips:**
- Use a high-resolution image
- Left side should be slightly darker (where text appears)
- Landscape orientation
- Show full vehicle or dramatic detail shot
- Good lighting is essential

---

## 2. Gallery Images

You need **at least 6 images** named `work-1.jpg` through `work-6.jpg`:

### work-1.jpg
- **Category:** Exterior
- **Size:** 1200x900px (4:3 aspect ratio)
- **Content:** Exterior detailing showcase (full vehicle, clean shine)

### work-2.jpg
- **Category:** Interior
- **Size:** 1200x900px
- **Content:** Interior deep clean result (seats, dashboard, etc.)

### work-3.jpg
- **Category:** Luxury
- **Size:** 1200x900px
- **Content:** Luxury vehicle (Tesla, BMW, Mercedes, etc.)

### work-4.jpg
- **Category:** Exterior
- **Size:** 1200x900px
- **Content:** Paint correction or ceramic coating results

### work-5.jpg
- **Category:** Interior
- **Size:** 1200x900px
- **Content:** Leather conditioning or detailed interior work

### work-6.jpg
- **Category:** Luxury
- **Size:** 1200x900px
- **Content:** Another luxury/Tesla vehicle

**You can add more images** (work-7.jpg, work-8.jpg, etc.) by following the same naming pattern and updating the HTML gallery section.

---

## 3. Open Graph Social Media Image

**File:** `og-image.jpg`  
**Size:** 1200x630px (exact size for Facebook/Twitter)  
**Purpose:** Shows when someone shares your website on social media  
**Content:** 
- Your logo or business name
- A clean, detailed car
- Text: "Splash Auto Detail - Chula Vista"

---

## Image Optimization

Before adding images to the folder:

1. **Compress them** for faster loading:
   - Use https://tinypng.com or https://squoosh.app
   - Target under 500KB for hero image
   - Target under 300KB each for gallery images

2. **Format:** Save as JPG (best for photos)

3. **Quality:** 80-85% is the sweet spot

4. **File names:** Use exactly the names listed above (case-sensitive)

## Photo Tips

### Good Photos Show:
✅ Before & after (side by side or separate)  
✅ Close-up detail work  
✅ Full vehicle shots with good lighting  
✅ Clean, professional angles  
✅ Variety of vehicles (sedans, SUVs, luxury)  

### Avoid:
❌ Blurry or dark photos  
❌ Cluttered backgrounds  
❌ Personal information (license plates can be blurred)  
❌ Low-resolution images  
❌ Over-edited/filtered photos  

---

## Need Help?

- Use your phone camera in good lighting
- Take photos from multiple angles
- Natural daylight works best
- Clean background (your shop, parking lot)
- Show the results, not the process

Once images are in place, refresh your website to see them appear automatically!
